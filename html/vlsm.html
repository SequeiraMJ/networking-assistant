Variable Length Network Mask

To perform VLSM subnetting, you only need to provide the initial IP address and required hosts per subnet. Just click on "Add Input" to add as many form input fields as you need and then click on "Calculate" to get the result. Remember, you have to enter host per subnet from highest to lowest.
